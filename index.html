About ELUXIOR

ELUXIOR is a sourcing and buying office dedicated to connecting international buyers with reliable, high-quality manufacturers across North Africa. We support brands, retailers, and distributors with product development, supplier selection, quality control, and supply chain coordination.

Our work is based on transparency, on-the-ground expertise, and long-term relationships with factories in Tunisia, Morocco, and Egypt. We aim to make sourcing simple, efficient, and dependable.

Regions We Cover

Local presence and factory relationships across key North African sourcing hubs.

Tunisia

Strong capabilities in handicrafts, olive wood products, apparel, and natural-fiber items. Ideal for home & lifestyle collections with an artisanal touch.

Morocco

Leather goods, ceramics, footwear, and fashion accessories produced by experienced manufacturers combining tradition and modern design.

Egypt

Competitive sourcing for cotton textiles, home linens, glassware, and selected food and natural products, with strong export know-how.

Asia Sourcing With SOTA

To complement our North African base, ELUXIOR partners with SOTA, a sourcing and design agency headquartered in Thailand. SOTA operates across Southeast Asia and China, including Vietnam, Cambodia, and surrounding regions.

Together, we offer:

  • Extended sourcing coverage across Africa and Asia
  • Design and product development support
  • Factory compliance and capacity assessments
  • End-to-end coordination from sampling to shipment

This partnership allows our clients to work with a single point of contact while benefiting from a wider, reliable supplier network.
